What is the web made of

Have you ever wondered how websites and web applications are created and maintained? Web development is the field that deals with just that. In our modern world, having an online presence is incredibly important, whether you're a business or an individual. Web development involves a lot of different skills, like programming, web design, server management, and database administration. Even if you're not familiar with these terms, don't worry! We'll explain everything in simple language. From basic websites to complex web applications, web development is constantly evolving and plays a huge role in shaping the online world that we use every day. In this article, we'll go over the basics of web development and help you understand what it's all about.
